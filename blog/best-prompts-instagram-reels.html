üèãÔ∏è Fitness & Health Reels

1. Workout Tutorial Reel

Write a 30-second Instagram Reel script about "5 Common Workout Mistakes Beginners Make." Structure it with:

  • Attention-grabbing hook in the first 3 seconds
  • List each mistake with a quick fix (5 seconds each)
  • Strong call-to-action at the end
  • Suggestions for on-screen text overlays
  • Recommended transitions between mistakes

Make it educational yet entertaining. Target beginners who are intimidated by the gym.

2. Fitness Transformation Story

Create a 45-second motivational Instagram Reel script showcasing a fitness transformation journey. Include:

  • Emotional hook about "where I started"
  • 3 key struggles I overcame
  • The turning point moment
  • Results and lessons learned
  • Inspiring call-to-action for viewers

Suggest music style (motivational/uplifting) and visual progression from "before" to "after" shots.

üëó Fashion & Style Reels

3. Outfit Styling Challenge

Write a 20-second Instagram Reel script for "3 Ways to Style One Basic Piece." Structure:

  • Hook: "You only need ONE [item] for 3 different looks"
  • Show each styling option with quick transitions (6-7 seconds each)
  • Occasion labels for each look (casual, work, date night)
  • End with "Which look is your favorite? Comment below!"

Suggest trending audio and text overlay placements. Make it fast-paced and visually appealing.

4. Fashion Trend Explainer

Create a 35-second Reel script about "Fashion Trends You Need to Know This Season." Include:

  • Catchy hook: "If you're not wearing these, you're already behind"
  • 5-6 trending items/styles (5 seconds each)
  • Where to buy affordable versions
  • How to style for different body types

Make it educational and shopping-focused. Include swipe-up or link-in-bio CTA.

üç≥ Food & Cooking Reels

5. Quick Recipe Tutorial

Write a 45-second Instagram Reel script for a quick and easy recipe. Structure:

  • Hook: "This takes 5 minutes and tastes better than takeout"
  • Ingredient list flash (3 seconds)
  • Step-by-step cooking process (30 seconds)
  • Final reveal with taste reaction
  • Recipe link in bio CTA

Suggest trending audio, text overlays for each step, and close-up shot moments. Make it mouth-watering and achievable.

6. Food Hack Compilation

Create a 30-second Reel script: "3 Food Hacks That Will Change Your Life." Include:

  • Viral-worthy opening hook
  • Each hack demonstrated in 8-10 seconds
  • Before/after comparisons
  • "Which one are you trying first?" engagement question

Make it surprising and useful. Focus on hacks people haven't seen before.

7. Business Tips Reel

Write a 40-second Reel script: "3 Business Lessons I Wish I Knew at 25." Structure:

  • Hook with personal credibility (revenue, years in business)
  • Lesson #1 with real example (12 seconds)
  • Lesson #2 with real example (12 seconds)
  • Lesson #3 with real example (12 seconds)
  • CTA: "Save this for later" + follow

Make it authentic and relatable. Include text overlays summarizing each lesson.

8. Income Transparency Reel

Create a 35-second Reel: "How I Make $10K/Month as a Creator - Full Breakdown." Include:

  • Attention-grabbing income reveal hook
  • Income breakdown by source (sponsorships, products, services)
  • Time investment for each income stream
  • Honest about what it took to get there
  • Link to detailed guide/newsletter

Build trust through transparency. Suggest professional aesthetic and on-screen graphics.
